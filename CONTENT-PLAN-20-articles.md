# GrowthForge Content Plan: 20 SEO/GEO Articles

## Research Summary

Three deep research streams identified the following landscape:

**Market context:** 37% of consumers now start searches with AI instead of Google. AI-referred sessions grew 527% YoY. Reddit is the #1 AI-cited source (40% of AI responses). YouTube has overtaken Reddit as the most-cited social source in AI responses (39.2% vs 20.3%). 83% of searches with AI Overviews end with zero clicks.

**Content gaps identified:**
1. Nobody combines monitoring + seeding + reporting into one framework
2. Industry-specific GEO guides don't exist (law firms, SaaS, e-commerce, local)
3. The Reddit → AI citation pipeline is barely articulated
4. Agency-operator content barely exists — everyone writes for in-house marketers
5. Practitioner playbooks with real before/after data are almost nonexistent
6. "Citation seeding platform" and "AI visibility for agencies" are unclaimed terms
7. AI citation decay (48-72 hour visibility loss) has no deep-dive content
8. YouTube's role in AI citations is massively underreported

**Key stats to weave into content:**
- 527% YoY growth in AI-referred sessions
- Reddit appears in 68% of AI answers; 95% of product-review queries
- Schema markup gives 2.5x higher chance of AI citation
- Only 6% of AI brand mentions result in actual recommendations
- Brand mentions correlate 3x more with AI visibility than backlinks
- 90% of citations driving LLM visibility come from earned media
- Content freshness: 76.4% of ChatGPT's top-cited pages updated within 30 days
- Only 11% of cited domains appear across multiple AI platforms

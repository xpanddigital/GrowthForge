# AI-Generated Review Responses

Every review deserves a response — but writing personalized replies for dozens of reviews per month is time-consuming. MentionLayer generates professional, on-brand responses that address each reviewer's specific comments.

## Why Responding to Every Review Matters

### Positive Reviews
A thoughtful thank-you response:
- Reinforces the positive experience
- Encourages the reviewer to return
- Shows potential customers (and AI models) that you value feedback
- Creates additional positive text content associated with your brand

### Negative Reviews
A professional, empathetic response:
- Can resolve the issue and change the reviewer's mind
- Shows other readers that you take problems seriously
- Limits the damage of the negative review in AI model assessments
- Creates an opportunity to demonstrate your values under pressure

### Neutral Reviews
Even 3-star reviews deserve acknowledgment:
- Shows you're responsive across the board, not just cherry-picking
- Can tip a neutral reviewer toward positive in future interactions

<Callout type="info">
AI models factor in response patterns. A brand that responds to 90% of reviews looks more engaged and trustworthy than one that only responds to 5-star reviews. Consistent response rates across all sentiment levels signal a brand that genuinely cares.
</Callout>

## How Response Generation Works

For each review, MentionLayer generates a response that:

1. **Acknowledges** the reviewer's specific feedback (not a generic "thanks for your review!")
2. **Addresses** any issues or concerns they raised
3. **Matches** your brand's tone guidelines
4. **Includes** relevant next steps (for negative reviews: how to resolve, who to contact)
5. **Keeps it professional** — no defensive language, no excuses

The AI reads the full review text and crafts a response that references specific details the reviewer mentioned. This is critical — generic responses are worse than no response because they signal you didn't actually read the feedback.

## Response Templates by Sentiment

### Positive Review Response
- Thank the reviewer by name (if available)
- Reference the specific feature or experience they mentioned
- Express genuine appreciation
- Optionally mention an upcoming feature or improvement related to their comment
- Keep it concise — 2-4 sentences

### Negative Review Response
- Acknowledge the frustration without being defensive
- Apologize for the specific experience, not generically
- Explain what you're doing to address the issue (if applicable)
- Provide a direct contact for resolution (email or phone)
- Keep it professional and empathetic — 3-5 sentences

### Neutral Review Response
- Thank them for the balanced feedback
- Acknowledge both the positive and constructive parts
- Address any specific concerns they raised
- Invite them to reach out if they'd like to discuss further
- Keep it warm — 2-3 sentences

## The Review-Edit-Post Workflow

### Step 1: Generate
Select unresponded reviews in the feed. Click "Generate Responses." MentionLayer creates a draft response for each one.

### Step 2: Review
Read each generated response against the original review. Check that:
- It references the reviewer's specific comments
- The tone matches your brand
- Negative review responses don't sound defensive
- The factual content is accurate

### Step 3: Edit
Adjust anything that feels off. Add personal touches you know about the situation. Remove anything that sounds too corporate.

### Step 4: Post
Copy the response and post it on the review platform. MentionLayer marks the review as "Responded" with the response date.

<Callout type="warning">
Never post a response without reading it first. AI-generated responses occasionally miss context or misinterpret the reviewer's tone. A quick review takes 30 seconds and prevents embarrassing mistakes.
</Callout>

## Response Time Targets

For maximum impact on both customer experience and AI perception:

- **Negative reviews:** Respond within 24 hours
- **Positive reviews:** Respond within 48 hours
- **Neutral reviews:** Respond within 72 hours

Faster is always better, but these targets balance responsiveness with practicality.

# Thread Discovery Explained

Before you can seed a citation, you need to find the right threads. Not every Reddit post or Quora question is worth responding to — discovery is about surfacing the ones that will actually move your AI visibility.

## Two Discovery Methods

MentionLayer uses two complementary methods to find citation-worthy threads:

### 1. SERP Scanning
This is the primary method. MentionLayer searches Google for your keywords and collects Reddit, Quora, and Facebook Group threads that appear in the results.

Why SERP scanning works: if a thread ranks on Google page 1 for a buying-intent keyword, it's almost certainly being referenced by AI models that pull from Google's index (Perplexity, Gemini, Google AI Overviews).

For each keyword, MentionLayer runs multiple search variations:
- `site:reddit.com {keyword}`
- `{keyword} reddit`
- `site:quora.com {keyword}`
- `{keyword} facebook group`

This catches threads that rank for your keyword across all three platforms.

### 2. AI Probing
This is the secret weapon. MentionLayer sends buying-intent prompts to AI models (primarily Perplexity, which returns source URLs) and extracts the threads being cited.

This finds threads that SERP scanning might miss — threads that don't rank highly in Google but are being cited by AI models through other paths.

Together, SERP scanning and AI probing give you comprehensive coverage of threads that matter for AI visibility.

<Callout type="tip">
AI probing is especially valuable for finding threads cited by Perplexity, which sometimes references pages that aren't in Google's top 10 but are relevant to the query context.
</Callout>

## The Opportunity Score

Every discovered thread gets an opportunity score from 0-100, calculated from four signals:

### Relevance (35% weight)
How closely does this thread relate to your brand and keywords? A thread titled "Best CRM for small agencies" is highly relevant if you sell a CRM. A thread about "CRM industry news" is less relevant even if it mentions CRMs.

MentionLayer's AI reads the thread title, body, and top comments to assess relevance against your brand brief.

### Google Authority (25% weight)
Where does this thread rank in Google? Position 1 = maximum authority. Position 50 = minimal. Threads in the top 5 are cited most frequently by AI models.

### Recency (20% weight)
When was the thread posted? Recent threads are more valuable:
- Under 7 days: 100 points
- Under 30 days: 80 points
- Under 90 days: 50 points
- Under 1 year: 30 points
- Older: 10 points

Older threads can still be valuable if they rank well and are still active, but fresh threads give you the biggest window of opportunity.

### Engagement (20% weight)
How active is the thread? Comment count is the primary signal:
- 100+ comments: 100 points
- 20-100 comments: 75 points
- 5-20 comments: 50 points
- Under 5 comments: 20 points

High-engagement threads are both more visible to AI models and more likely to have your response seen and upvoted by real users.

## Automatic Thread Routing

Based on the opportunity score, threads are automatically routed:

| Score | Status | Action |
|-------|--------|--------|
| 70-100 | Queued | High-priority — ready for response generation |
| 40-69 | Classified | Review manually — may be worth targeting |
| 0-39 | Auto-skipped | Low opportunity — not worth the effort |

You can always override the automatic routing. A thread scored at 35 might be perfect for your specific niche — trust your judgment.

## Deduplication

MentionLayer automatically deduplicates threads. If the same Reddit post is discovered through both SERP scanning and AI probing, it appears once in your queue with both discovery sources noted.

Deduplication uses a content hash of the normalized title and URL, so the same thread won't clutter your queue even if it's found multiple times across different keywords.

## Discovery Scheduling

Discovery runs can be triggered two ways:

- **Automatic:** Scheduled to run twice per week (Tuesday and Friday mornings by default). This keeps your thread queue fresh without manual intervention.
- **Manual:** Click "Run Scan" in the Citation Engine to trigger an immediate discovery run for the current client.

Each run processes all active keywords for the selected client and typically takes 5-15 minutes depending on keyword count.

## In MentionLayer

The thread queue in Citation Engine shows all discovered threads with their opportunity scores, platforms, and status. Filter by score, platform, intent, or date to find your best targets.
